16 Oct. 2025

Live recording of Mahler’s Symphony No. 8 with James

Kolinberg Philharmonic and James' live recording of Mahler's Symphony No. 8 will be released by Decca Classics on 7 November 2025. Captured during the 2025 Mahler Festival in Amsterdam, the album will be available digitally worldwide.
